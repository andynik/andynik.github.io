#### Kvanta STEM

In 2016, I co-founded [_Kvanta_](https://kvanta.xyz) and directed yearly STEM programs, reaching **over 1,000 young learners** and inspiring their interest in STEM. 

**More than 5,000 learning hours** have been delivered through weekly lessons and over 10 educational boot camps — both in-person and online (with up to 200 participants) — by our dedicated team of volunteer instructors and educators.

**Over $150,000** for nurturing young STEM talents was secured in funding through grant applications and partnerships with educational institutions, IT companies and educators, including Emergent Ventures, XTX Markets, GlobalLogic, Admixer, and others, to support ongoing project development.

As a result of our commitment, many Kvanta graduates have continued their passion for science -- participating in domestic and international top-level competitions (IMO, IOI, EGMO etc.), starting academic journeys at the universities worldwide, and building their own path to glory.
